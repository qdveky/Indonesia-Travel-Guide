Learn Basic Bahasa Indonesia

Start your journey in learning Bahasa Indonesia with these essential words and phrases that are commonly used in daily conversations.

Words

  • Yes: Ya
  • No: Tidak
  • Please: Tolong
  • Thank you: Terima kasih
  • Sorry: Maaf
  • Good morning: Selamat pagi
  • Good night: Selamat malam
  • Water: Air
  • Food: Makanan
  • Friend: Teman
  • Love: Cinta
  • Happy: Bahagia
  • Sad: Sedih
  • Big: Besar
  • Small: Kecil
  • New: Baru
  • Old: Tua
  • Good: Baik
  • Bad: Buruk
  • Hot: Panas

Phrases

  • Hello, how are you?: Halo, apa kabar?
  • What is your name?: Siapa nama Anda?
  • I don't understand: Saya tidak mengerti
  • Where is the bathroom?: Di mana kamar mandi?
  • How much does this cost?: Berapa harganya?
  • I would like to order food: Saya ingin memesan makanan
  • Can you help me?: Bisakah Anda membantu saya?
  • I'm looking for a hotel: Saya mencari hotel
  • Goodbye, see you later: Selamat tinggal, sampai jumpa lagi
