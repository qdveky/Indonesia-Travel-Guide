Welcome to Indonesia!

A Journey of Discovery and Adventure

Dear Exchange Students,

Selamat datang di Indonesia! (Welcome to Indonesia!)

We are thrilled to welcome you to the vibrant and diverse archipelago of Indonesia, where ancient traditions meet modern wonders. This travel guide has been crafted with care to ensure your time in Jakarta, the bustling capital, and beyond is filled with unforgettable experiences and meaningful connections.

About This Guide

Purpose: This guide is designed specifically for exchange students like you, who have chosen Jakarta as their study destination. Whether you're here for a semester or a year, we want to make sure you make the most of your time in this beautiful country.

Destinations: Explore the top 10 destinations carefully curated for you. From the bustling streets of Jakarta to the serene beauty of Bali, each location offers a unique glimpse into Indonesia's rich culture, history, and natural wonders.

Your Indonesian Adventure Awaits

Jakarta - A City of Contrasts

Get ready to dive into the heart of Indonesia's capital, where modern skyscrapers stand alongside historic temples. From vibrant markets to serene parks, Jakarta has something for everyone.

Yogyakarta - Cultural Epicenter

Discover the soul of Java in Yogyakarta, a city where ancient traditions and modern arts converge. Explore the iconic Borobudur Temple and catch a traditional Ramayana dance performance.

Bali - Island Paradise

Escape to the enchanting island of Bali, where lush landscapes, vibrant ceremonies, and pristine beaches await. Unwind in Ubud's tranquility or ride the waves in Seminyak.

... and many more destinations waiting to be explored!
